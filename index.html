About Me

Hello! I’m Reuel Abletia, a Software QA Engineer with a strong focus on delivering stable, reliable, and user-ready systems. I take pride in my attention to detail and my ability to validate features against real business and client requirements. Graduating Cum Laude shaped my discipline, critical thinking, and commitment to quality in everything I do.

For nearly three years, I’ve worked hands-on in testing web and backoffice systems, performing acceptance, exploratory, regression, sanity, and end-to-end testing. I’ve validated player transactions, bonuses, rewards, and system configurations, ensuring accurate data flow from backoffice to web. I collaborate closely with developers and support teams to reproduce issues, verify fixes, and help ensure smooth and stable releases.

Beyond testing, I enjoy exploring UI/UX improvements, improving system reliability, and learning better ways to document and communicate quality. I believe quality assurance is not just about finding defects, but about protecting the user experience and building trust in every release.
