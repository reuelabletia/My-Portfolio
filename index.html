About Me

Hello! I'm Reuel Abletia, a dedicated Software QA Specialist with a passion for ensuring seamless and secure digital experiences. My journey in QA has been driven by a meticulous eye for detail and a commitment to delivering high-quality products. I graduated with Cum Laude honors, which instilled in me a strong foundation for excellence and critical thinking.

For almost two years, I’ve tested a wide range of systems and websites, making sure they work well, are secure, and give users a smooth experience. I’ve worked with projects built using PHP (Laravel) and Node.js on the backend, with MySQL for managing data. On the frontend, many of the systems I tested used Vue/Nuxt, styled with Tailwind CSS. I’ve also been involved in testing WordPress and Shopify projects, focusing on how reliable, fast, and user-friendly they are.

When I’m not breaking systems for a living, I’m exploring UI designs, delving into speed optimization challenges, or contributing to open-source testing communities. I believe that quality assurance is not just about finding bugs, but about building trust and ensuring user satisfaction.
